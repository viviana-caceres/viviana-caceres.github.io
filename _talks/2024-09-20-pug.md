Primordial Universe and Gravitation (PUG) seminars are weekly student-organized chalkboard talks aimed at graduate students in the Institute for Gravitation and the Cosmos. This PUG talk introduced quasinormal modes of black holes and discussed how they were used to test general relativity with GW150914.
