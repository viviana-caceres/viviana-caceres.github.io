I was honored to receive the NSF-GRFP in 2025, a year when the NSF [made notably few award offers](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-025-01098-9). If you're applying for the fellowship and are looking for example statements, please feel free to send me an email and I'll be happy to share my research and personal statements with you! 
Learn more about the program at the [NSF GRFP website](https://www.nsfgrfp.org/).
